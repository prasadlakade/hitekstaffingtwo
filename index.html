Who we are

HiTek Staffing, headquartered in Virginia, is one of the premier independent providers of engineering & information technology services to the IT industry. We are also a full-service IT recruiting firm and a diversity-certified, woman-owned, disadvantaged enterprise small business.  HiTek Staffing was formed to provide the highest quality solutions while maintaining a fine balance between cost, quality, and timeline. The HiTek Staffing team has extensive experience in information technology services project planning, execution and staffing.
